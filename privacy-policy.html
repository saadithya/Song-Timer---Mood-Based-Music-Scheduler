Privacy Policy for Song Timer - Mood Based Music Scheduler

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

Thank you for using Song Timer - Mood Based Music Scheduler (“the Extension”). Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the Extension.

1. Information We Collect

  • Local Data: The Extension saves user-created timers, schedules, and configuration settings locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage API.
  • No Personal Data: The Extension does not request, collect, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) unless you voluntarily include such information in a timer name or note.

2. How Information Is Used

  • Timer and scheduling data is used solely to provide the Extension’s functionality (e.g., starting music playback at a scheduled time).
  • Host permissions for YouTube and Spotify are used exclusively to allow playback control when timers are triggered.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data remains stored locally within your browser and is not automatically transmitted to any external server.
  • If you choose to export settings or backups, the data is only saved to your device via Chrome’s download feature.
  • Because no external transmission occurs, your data remains secure on your device.

4. Data Sharing

  • The Extension does not share, sell, or transmit your data to third parties.
  • No advertising or analytics tools are integrated into the Extension.

5. Permissions Justification

  • Storage: To save timers and configuration locally.
  • Alarms: To schedule music playback at specified times.
  • YouTube & Spotify Host Permissions: Required to enable playback control on these sites only.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Extension is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Extension after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
