Privacy Policy for Song Timer Extension

We respect your privacy. This extension is designed with a privacy-first approach:

  • Data Collection: The extension does not collect or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII).
  • Local Storage: Timers and configuration settings are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.
  • No Third Parties: Data is not shared with or transmitted to any third-party services.
  • Host Permissions: Access to YouTube and Spotify is required only to enable playback control when timers are triggered. No other sites are accessed.
  • Data Security: Since no data is transmitted externally, your information remains secure within your device.
